## Want to make your own pet?

Use the *method*, not the character. The toolkit this pet was built with —
[pet-forge](https://github.com/SANABI-LL/pet-forge-2) — is designed for exactly

Lawrie is the one thing it asks you not to do; making your own is the whole
point.
